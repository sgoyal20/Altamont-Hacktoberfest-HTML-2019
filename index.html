About this Site

Information and Background

Here at the Altamont School in Birmingham, AL, we've been doing Computer Science for the past 4 years. This is our second year participating in the GitHub Hacktoberfest. The purpose of this site is to deomonstrate how students can use what they know about HTML and CSS to contribute to a common repository and website. If you want to contribute, just follow the instructions in the ReadMe. I look forward to seeing what gets created!
